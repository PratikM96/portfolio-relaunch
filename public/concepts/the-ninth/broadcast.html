Motion, applied

How the package moves

Rise

Enter from below or the edge

Overlays drift in the way you rise through cloud. Nothing snaps or drops. The lower-third slides, the sting scales up gently.

Ease

240 to 500ms, no bounce

Broadcast speed without the frenzy. Controlled easing reads as premium, which is the brand's whole register at pace.

Summit

Frost is rationed

The iridescent IX appears only on the moment sting and the Inner Nine shout-out. Everywhere else the package stays in calm glass and blue.

Production note: this is the design and motion logic, built in HTML so it animates and records for review. Final broadcast delivery is After Effects over real LCS and VCT footage, with Runway and Veo used to previs the complex transitions before finishing by hand.
