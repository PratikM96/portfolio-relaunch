Speculative concept · Phase 5 · social content engine

One match in, a week out.THE NINTH · SOCIAL CONTENT ENGINE

Not a posting schedule, a system. One match produces a week of cross-platform output across a managed account portfolio, drafted by the same AI that powers the app's clipper and steered by a human for taste and voice. The result reaches far more fans than a single official account at the same production cost. Vertical-first, because 56% of esports viewing is mobile.

The pipeline

From one match to a portfolio

The engine is a funnel. The clipper built in Phase 3 is its source: each broadcast day yields 10 to 25 clip-worthy moments. The AI drafts platform-native variants per template, a human approves and tunes voice, and the set fans out across the account portfolio on an event-driven cadence.

The model

A managed account portfolio

The strategic shift: orgs no longer run one official account, they run a portfolio of them. Algorithmic coverage from many accounts reaches 5 to 20x a single account at similar cost. Each tier has its own role, polish, and cadence.

Hero accounts
1 to 2 per platform
The official voice. Highest polish: results, announcements, the title bumper, brand films. Calm and premium, never spammy.

Template set

Vertical-first for mobile, each also exporting square for feed. Every template carries the altitude system, with frost rationed to member moments. The AI fills them and the templates hold the brand, so output stays on-system at volume.

The logic

Where AI assists, where taste leads

AI does the volume

Detection of moments, first-draft cut-downs, per-platform reframing, caption variants, and alt-text. The work that does not scale by hand is exactly the work the template plus model handles, which is what makes a portfolio of this size affordable.

Input: one match. Output: a draft set, tagged and templated.

Humans steer the brand

Voice, the dry wit, spoiler timing, what not to post, and which moment actually deserves the hero account. Nothing publishes without a human gate. The AI proposes and a person with taste disposes, which is how the calm-premium register survives at volume.

The approval gate is the brand's quality control, not a formality.
