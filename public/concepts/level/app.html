Level App, phone-first
06 / Product

The product carries the position.

A phone-first walk through the core flow: find a market, understand it, stake with friction, and stay in control. The responsible-use system from the spec is built into the screens, not bolted on after. Amber stays disciplined, outcomes stay neutral, and the limit travels with the user the whole way.

Explain this marketclose
What does 62% actually mean here?
It means the market, the crowd of people staking, currently puts the home win at a 62 in 100 chance. It is not a guarantee, and it moves as money and news come in.
It resolves on the final score at full time. A draw or a loss both count as No.
Why: home unbeaten in 6, key striker fit, away side on a back-to-back. Source lines update live.
