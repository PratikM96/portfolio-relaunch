08 / AI in the product

Understand it first. Then stake.

This is the assistant at the center of Level. Pick a market and ask it anything in plain language. It answers the way the brand talks, and it is also the gate. The stake button stays locked until you have the basics: what the number means and what it resolves on. It is a scripted in-character engine, not a live model, built to show the interaction.

What this proves

Legibility as harm reduction, made interactive. In a category that hides the odds behind a polished interface, Level puts a teacher in front of the stake. The gate is the point: you cannot stake on a market you have not understood.

The gate

The stake button only unlocks once you have asked what the probability means and what the market resolves on. It is a one-time gate per market type in the real product, not a nag.

In Level's voice

  • Plain language, never jargon or hype.
  • Names the risk honestly, never sells a sure thing.
  • Shows the evidence behind the number, with the fee up front.
  • Calm. It will tell you when something is not worth it.

Try: "what does 62% mean", "what does it resolve on", "is this a sure thing", "what are the fees".
