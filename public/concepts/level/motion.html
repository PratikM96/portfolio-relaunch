Level Motion explainers
07 / Motion

Motion that teaches, and settles.

Four live explainers. They do the work the brand promises, making a probability legible, and they move the way Level moves: easing to rest, never flashing, with the same restraint on a loss as a win. They are built to loop quietly, and they honor reduced-motion settings.

Settle to level

Transitions decelerate and come to a calm stop, like a bubble finding level. Nothing snaps or bounces.

The index moves last

When data changes, the amber marker eases to its new place so the shift is legible, not alarming.

No celebration

A win and a loss resolve with identical restraint. No confetti, no reward for the act of staking.
